White Noise Oasis

"White Noise Oasis" is taken from White Noise + Oasis. "White noise" refers to sound signals that contain all audible frequencies and are often used to help people focus, relax or fall asleep. "Oasis" literally means a fertile place in the desert. It means a peaceful or comfortable place amidst the hustle and bustle.
